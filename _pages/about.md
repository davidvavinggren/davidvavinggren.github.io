I graduated from [Y-linjen](https://liu.se/utbildning/program/6cyyy) at [Linköping University](https://liu.se/) in 2024 with a Master of Science in Engineering. This included a bachelor's degree in Applied Physics and Electrical Engineering, and a master's degree in Data Science and Machine Intelligence.

In January 2025, I started my journey as a PhD student at the [Division of Systems and Control](https://www.uu.se/en/department/information-technology/research/control-and-dynamical-systems/division-of-systems-and-control), which is a part of the [Department of Information Technology](https://www.uu.se/en/department/information-technology) at [Uppsala University](https://www.uu.se/). I am supervised by [Antônio Horta Ribeiro](https://antonior92.github.io/), and co-supervised by [André Teixeira](https://www.andre-teixeira.eu/) and [Dave Zachariah](https://www.uu.se/en/contact-and-organisation/staff?query=N13-1398). My research lies in the intersection between statistics and machine learning, where I currently work on adversarial training for linear models.
